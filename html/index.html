POWERS THE GRID WITH OUR UNIQUE OPERATING SYSTEM

Our open-source, stateless and lightweight operating system, Zero-OS was built from a Linux kernel and makes servers trustworthy and more performant. We call these servers ‘3Nodes’ and their owners ‘Farmers’. Together, Farmers form the ThreeFold Grid - the world's largest peer-to-peer network.

INTRODUCING AUTONOMOUS AND DECENTRALIZED COMPUTE AND STORAGE

3Nodes form a global grid of autonomous and decentralized storage and compute. Connections between 3Nodes are fully encrypted, and a Smart Contract for IT technology manages all workloads. This unique architecture brings the security and reliability of a blockchain to high-end server technology.
